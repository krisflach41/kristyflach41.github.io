About Me

I’m Kristy Flach—a Certified Mortgage Advisor, mortgage strategist, and educator who believes your mortgage should be a financial tool, not just a transaction.

With 17 years of underwriting experience and a background as a licensed Realtor, I structure loans for smooth approvals and smart long-term outcomes. I focus on education, strategy, and clarity—so you know what you're doing, and why.

When I'm not helping clients, you’ll find me outdoors with my Westie, Gus, or enjoying time with family back in my home state of Ohio.
